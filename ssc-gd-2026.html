How to Download SSC GD City Intimation Slip 2026

City Slip will be available 8-10 days before the exam. Expected date: 17-20 April 2026

  1. Open the official SSC website
  2. Login with Registration Number and Password
  3. Click on "City Intimation Slip" link on the dashboard
  4. Download the PDF and check your exam city

How to Download SSC GD Admit Card 2026

  1. Go to the official SSC website
  2. Click on the "Admit Card" tab
  3. Select your SSC Region
  4. Enter Registration No + Date of Birth
  5. Download and print your Admit Card
