JKBOSE 10th Result 2026 Declared Today - Check Direct Link

BREAKING UPDATE: 21 April 2026, 11:00 AM
JKBOSE ne Class 10th Summer Zone ka result aaj jari kar diya hai.

Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education (JKBOSE) ne JK Board Class 10th Result 2026 for Summer Zone aaj, 21 April 2026 ko subah 11 AM baje online declare kar diya hai. 10,000+ students ka wait khatam ho gaya hai.

1. Kaunsa Result Aaya Hai?

Ye Summer Zone Jammu division ka result hai jiska exam February-March 2026 mein hua tha. Winter Zone October-November 2025 session ka result pehle hi 14 January 2026 ko aa chuka tha jisme 84.6% pass percentage tha.

Session Exam Month Result Status Declared Date
Winter Zone Oct-Nov 2025 DECLARED ✅ 14 January 2026
Summer Zone Feb-March 2026 DECLARED ✅ 21 April 2026, 11 AM

2. JKBOSE 10th Result 2026 Kaise Check Kare - 3 Official Tarike

Official site kabhi-kabhi slow ho jati hai. Ye 3 tarike use karo:

  1. Website: jkresults.nic.in kholo → Class 10 link pe click karo → Roll Number + Registration Number daalo → Submit → Marksheet download kar lo.
  2. SMS: Mobile se type karo JKBOSE10 <space> Roll Number aur 5676750 pe bhej do. Result SMS mein aa jayega.
  3. DigiLocker: results.digilocker.gov.in ya DigiLocker app se login karke original digital marksheet download karo.

3. Important Warning: Fake Links Se Bachein

Result check karne ke liye koi paisa nahi lagta. Sirf .gov.in ya .nic.in wali website hi official hai. YouTube pe “Gazette PDF” wale fake link pe click mat karo. Online marksheet provisional hai, original marksheet 1 mahine mein school se milegi.
