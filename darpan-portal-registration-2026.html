Darpan Portal Online Registration 2026 - HS 1st Year Admission

By QuickApply.Com | Updated: April 18, 2026

The online application procedure has been implemented from 12th April 2026 and will last till 22nd April 2026. To avoid any last-minute obstacles, candidates should register themselves quickly from the Official Website.

📅 Darpan Admission 2026 Complete Schedule

Date Event
12/04/2026 to 22/04/2026 Submission of Online Applications by Applicants
23/04/2026 to 24/04/2026 Preparation of Merit/Waiting List by Institutions
25/04/2026 (10:00 AM) Publication of Provisional Selection/Waiting List (Valid up to 08/05/2026)
25/04/2026 to 27/04/2026 Acceptance of Admission Offer by Students
28/04/2026 to 08/05/2026 Confirmation of Admission by Institute

📄 Documents to be Uploaded with Online Form

Note: Students must keep the following listed documents ready in JPG, JPEG or PNG file format within maximum size of 1 MB during online application form submission.
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
  • HSLC / 10th Marksheet (Digital)
  • Caste Certificate (As applicable)
  • Disability Certificate (As applicable)
  • TGL or Ex-TGL Certificate (As applicable)
  • Gap Certificate (As applicable)

💰 Application Fee

No Application Fee. The Darpan Portal registration for HS Admission is completely free of cost.
