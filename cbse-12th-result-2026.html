CBSE Class 12th Result 2026 - Check Online @results.cbse.nic.in

By QuickApply.Com | Last Updated: April 20, 2026
Latest Update: CBSE Class 12th Result 2026 will be declared in the last week of April or first week of May 2026. CBSE has already declared Class 10th Result on April 15, 2026. Students keep your Roll Number, School Number, and Admit Card ID ready.

How to Check 2026 CBSE Class 12 Results Online

1. Step 1: Visit the official CBSE result website: results.cbse.nic.in

2. Step 2: On homepage, click on the link 'Senior School Certificate Examination (Class XII) Results 2026'

3. Step 3: Enter your credentials: Roll Number, School Number, Admit Card ID, and Security Pin

4. Step 4: Click 'Submit' and your CBSE 12th Marksheet 2026 will appear on screen

5. Step 5: Download the provisional marksheet and take a printout for future reference

Other Ways to Check CBSE 12th Result 2026

1. DigiLocker App/Website: Login to digilocker.gov.in → Go to "Pull Partner Documents" → Select CBSE → Enter Roll Number and Year 2026. Get original digital marksheet.

2. UMANG App: Download UMANG App from Play Store → Search "CBSE" → Select "Class XII Result 2026" → Enter details.

3. SMS Service: Type CBSE12 <Space> Roll Number and send to 7738299899. Result will come via SMS.

CBSE 12th Result 2026: Important Details

Board Name Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)
Exam Name Senior School Certificate Examination 2026
Exam Date 17 February to 10 April 2026
Result Date Last Week of April 2026 [Expected]
Passing Marks 33% in each subject (Theory + Practical)
Official Website results.cbse.nic.in
Important Note: The online result is provisional only. Original mark sheets and passing certificates should be collected from your respective schools after 10-15 days of result declaration. CBSE will not release any merit list or topper list to reduce unhealthy competition.
